About Me

I am a student from SMK Negeri 1 Pedan, majoring in Pengembangan Perangkat Lunak dan Gim (PPLG). I have a strong interest in creative things, even the simple ones. I enjoy seeing and doing something that has artistic or unique value, especially when working together with others.

The PPLG major focuses on the world of technology, programming, and software or game development. Through this field, I have learned a lot — from building applications, understanding programming logic, to designing digital interfaces. I’ve come to realize that creativity isn’t just about drawing or writing, but can also be expressed through code and technology.

Currently, I am doing an internship at PT Sandbox Indonesia Juara, located in North Klaten. During my internship, I have gained many new skills — from design and coding basics to technical abilities like typing faster and working more efficiently. I feel very lucky to be in such a supportive, friendly, and collaborative environment, which makes this internship one of the most valuable experiences for me.

In terms of personality, I’m still learning to be more independent and less dependent on others. I always try to respect and appreciate the people around me, both at school and at work.

Outside of studying, I have a few simple things I enjoy. When it comes to food, I like spicy, sweet, and savory flavors — something bold but delicious. For drinks, I prefer those that are not too sweet, just balanced. I also love healing in nature, such as visiting forests, lakes, or beaches, where the atmosphere feels fresh, calm, and relaxing.

As for my future plans, I want to take things step by step — work first, take an English course, and then continue to college. I believe every process I go through now will be a valuable foundation for my future.
